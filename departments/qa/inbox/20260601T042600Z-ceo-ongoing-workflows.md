## Policy
`confluence/workflows/` is a living library. It currently has 4 workflows (TDD, E2E-First, Spike, Creative Pipeline).

## Ongoing Responsibilities
- **QA, R&D, PM**: When you encounter a repeatable process, lesson learned, or new development pattern — write it as a workflow document in `confluence/workflows/`. Follow the existing format (When to Use, Process, Rules).
- **HR**: Whenever a new workflow is added to `confluence/workflows/`, integrate it into the relevant department's SYSTEM.md so they are required to follow it. HR is the bridge between workflow docs and department prompts.

This is not a one-time task. Every cycle, if there's a lesson worth formalizing, it becomes a workflow.
