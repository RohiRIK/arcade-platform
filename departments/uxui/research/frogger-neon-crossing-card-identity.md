# Research: Frogger Neon Crossing — Card Identity

**Date:** 2026-06-02
**Pivot:** arcade-evolution Phase 3

## Problem

Frogger game card uses the default banner with generic tinted background. It lacks a unique visual identity matching its Creative direction ("Neon Crossing" — cyberpunk rain-soaked city theme).

## Inspiration

- Creative direction spec: `departments/creative/artifacts/frogger-creative-direction.md`
- Color palette: dark wet asphalt `#0c0c1a`, player lime `#76ff03`, cyan platforms `#00e5ff`
- Theme: cyberpunk, rain, neon reflections, Blade Runner atmosphere

## Proposed Approach

Follow the established card identity pattern (gradient background + text-shadow glow on emoji):
- Gradient: `#0c0c1a` (dark wet asphalt) → `#1a1a2e` (standard card bg) at 135deg
- Glow: `#76ff03` (lime green drone) + `#00e5ff` (cyan sky-train) dual text-shadow
- Matches the rain-soaked neon city aesthetic from Creative direction
