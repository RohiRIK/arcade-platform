# Research: Tetris Cascade Card Identity

## Problem
Tetris has no unique card banner identity. It uses the default `#1a1a2e` background like non-migrated games. With Phase 3 approaching Tetris migration, the card should reflect the "Cascade" creative direction before R&D builds.

## Inspiration
- **Tetris creative direction** (`tetris-creative-direction.md`): Dark steel blue-black (`#0a0e14`), liquid-metal theme, industrial ambient mood. Piece colors: cyan, gold, purple, green, red, orange, blue.
- **Existing card identities**: Snake uses dark green gradient + green glow, Pong uses dark navy + cyan/orange dual glow, Breakout uses dark purple + magenta/purple glow. Each picks 1-2 signature colors from the game palette.
- **Industrial/metallic UIs**: Steel-blue tones with subtle cyan or warm amber accents evoke industrial feel without being cold.

## Proposed Approach
- Banner gradient: dark steel-black (`#0a0e14`) → standard card bg (`#1a1a2e`), matching the game's background
- Text glow: Cyan (`#00bcd4`) as primary — the I-piece color is iconic Tetris, plus a subtle warm gold (`#ffc107`) secondary glow to reflect the liquid-metal/alloy theme
- Pattern matches existing card identity format (135deg gradient + dual text-shadow glow)
